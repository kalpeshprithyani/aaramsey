Frequently Asked Questions

Do you repair equipments on doorstep??
It depends on the problem. If the problem is minor and the repair is possible at your place, it will be done there itself; otherwise, it’ll be taken to the service station.
What if the warranty on my equipments has expired?
If the warranty on your equipments has expired, the technicians will help you fix the equipments at a minimal cost. They will charge money as per our list price to replace the required damaged components with new ones.
Do you provide service for Crompton, Philips, Godrej or other reputed manufacturer repair/installation?
Yes. Our teams do provide repair, service and installation.
If spare parts are required for the repair, do I need to pay for them in advance?
If the professional is going to buy the materials, then you need to pay the money in advance so that the professional can make payments while buying the parts but if they have parts handy we will settle them in finale invoice. You will be given acknowledgement bills after the purchase.
Do you know about the Aaramsey Guarantee?
We share our service partner’s commitment to deliver the best customer experience, every time. To honor this commitment, we have launched The Aaramsey Insurance Protection Program. This program protects Aaramsey users against any loss during delivery of a service booked on Aaramsey. The sum assured is Rs. 5,000.
Please note that insurance coverage only applies to services paid for online before or after providing the service. Insurance coverage is not applicable on cash payments.

Aaramsey Insurance Protection Program applies to the following services:
  • Salon at Home for Men
  • Spa at Home for Women
  • Electrician, Plumber, and Carpenter services
  • All Home and Car Cleaning Services
  • Pest Control
  • Dry Cleaning
  • Or for other services for which we commit in description.

We do not extend the insurance protection program to customized service categories such as photographers, interior designers, packers and movers, where we allow customers to choose between various service professionals interested in their request.
In the unlikely event of any damages caused during the service delivery, you can reach us by using the ‘CONTACT US’ button located in your App or contact our help center.
The insurance coverage is not applicable on requests that have been cancelled.
How to book a service?
You can follow the following steps to book a service:
  • Search for the category you need.
  • Open the category and follow the instructions after proceeding.
  • Once you book the service, a professional should be assigned. A verified and trained professional will be assigned 1 hour before the booked time.
  • Assigned professionals will arrive at the address you provide and provide service.
How to cancel a booking?
If you want the service, but cannot avail it at the time for which you have booked the service, we recommend rescheduling the booking for a different time. If you want to cancel, follow the steps below.
  • Search the booking you want to cancel
  • Click cancel
  • Choose reason to cancel and submit
How to reschedule a booking?
You can follow the below steps to book a service to reschedule a booking for a different time. Search for the booking you want to reschedule Click on reschedule Select the preferred time slot if available and submit
How to know if my request is confirmed?
Once you make the booking, you will receive a confirmation via SMS or email or WhatsApp that will inform you that the booking has been accepted. A verified professional will be assigned to your booking 1 hour before service time.
How can I contact the professional?
Contact details of the professionals are shared with you in the Pending/Assigned page of your app. You can make a call from there.
When will the professional reach for delivering service?
Our professionals usually arrive within the selected time of booking. We track if the job is not started on time and our customer care partners automatically start working on the issue. Be assured that either a professional or an Aaramsey representative will contact you in case of job can"t get started in time. If you face any problem, booking for which you can raise a complaint on our app.
How can I rate and review the service of a professional?
After each service is completed, you will be asked to rate the professional. You would also be allowed to leave a feedback for the service and suggest improvements on various parameters.
How can I book the same professional again who delivered my last service?
You can ask for your preferred professional from our help section or from a call to our support team. In order to book your preferred professional, select the services you want to book and select the address. Please remember that on Aaramsey, professionals have choice to either accept your job or not take it up according to their preference.
How to pay for the services?
You can pay for the service either before or after service delivery. To pay after the service delivery, please ask the professional to close complaint and generate bill and you can pay online through our different payment mode, In case of cash we can verify if from technician by OTP .
Note that Aaramsey Insurance Protection and Warranty is only applicable on payments made online only.

You can pay using the following methods
  • Debit (ATM) cards or credit cards
  • Net banking of all popular banks
  • For India only, wallets like PayTM & Phonepe
  • Cash
How to use Aaramsey credits?
Aaramsey Credits can only be used on the Aaramsey app. Aaramsey credits can only be used in categories which accept online payment while paying for the services online. Aaramsey credits can only be used while paying for the service online.
Refund policy
On cancellation of a request, the refund will be automatically initiated into your original payment method within 5 -7 business days. For example, if you paid for a service via credit card, the amount will be credit back into your credit card.
Does Aaramsey provide any service guarantee?
Yes, we do provide a 30 days service guarantee and Insurance of upto Rs. 5,000/- against any damages caused. Service guarantee varies from service to service. Please check the details while booking a service. The service guarantees are offered on all bookings that are paid for online only.
